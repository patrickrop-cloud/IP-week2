Akan name calculator

Hello, welcome to this simple webpage. This is a project to calculate an Akan name(origin names from Ghanian culture). Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names.

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female

      Sunday: Akosua
      Monday: Adwoa
      Tuesday: Abenaa
      Wednesday: Akua
      Thursday: Yaa
      Friday: Afua
      Saturday: Ama
